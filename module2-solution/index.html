My Name

Ogechi

The name Ogechi is a native name, borne by people from Igbo tribe in Eastern Nigeria.It is a two syllabic words "Oge and Chi", Oge means Time while Chi is a shortened name for Chukwunna which stands for God. The two names combined is Ogechi which means God's time.

Promise

Promise is an English word which means a declaration or assurance that one will do something or that a particular thing will happen. It can also mean legally binding declaration that gives the person to whom it is made a right to expect or to claim the performance or forbearance of a specified act. In summary, a promise is the quality of potential excellence.

Onah

Just like Ogechi, Onah is a native name which stems from Igala land in Nigeria. Igala belongs to the Noerth central zone but have boundries with some parts of the Eastern part. It is said that the migration from both sides led to the adaption of languages from both. While Ona is a type of yam in Igbo language, it stands for Landlord in Igala and is also answered widely in Igala land.
